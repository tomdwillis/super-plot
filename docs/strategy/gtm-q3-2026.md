## 1. ICP: Who We're Selling To

**Primary:** Active land investor ("Wilco Land" profile)
- Evaluates 1–10 vacant parcels per month
- Current research time: 4–8 hours per parcel (manual, fragmented across 5+ sources)
- Annual research spend per deal: $50–$500 (Land Academy benchmarks)
- Decision context: Evaluating whether to acquire, develop, or pass — often on-site with phone in hand

**Secondary (Q4 consideration, not Q3):** Land broker/agent seeking justification tools for buyers

**Where ICPs currently find parcel intelligence:**

| Need | Current Tools | Pain Point |
|---|---|---|
| Ownership / assessor data | County assessor sites | Free but fragmented; inconsistent formats |
| Comparable sales | AcreValue, LandWatch, manual MLS | Limited rural comps; asking vs. sold bias |
| Environmental (wetlands, flood) | FEMA maps, NWI (manual) | 3–4 separate logins; no integration |
| Zoning | County GIS portals | County-specific terminology; no standard output |
| Parcel geometry | Regrid, county GIS | Paid or variable quality |

**Core job-to-be-done:** Eliminate fragmentation — replace 5+ tool logins with one 5-minute workflow.

## 4. Top 3 Risks (Specific)

### Risk 1: Free-to-Paid Conversion Drops Below 10%

**Why it's real:** Land investor communities are price-sensitive; county sites are free. "I can spend 2 extra hours and not pay $59" is a real objection, especially for investors evaluating thin-margin deals.

**Mitigation:**
- Enter at $29 beta price (not $59) for first paid report; anchor against $59 regular
- 3-pack bundle ($99 for 3 reports) to increase transaction size and reduce friction
- Money-back guarantee: "If you don't save 4+ hours on your next deal, full refund"
- Track free-to-paid by channel; pause any channel below 8% conversion

---

### Risk 2: Early Report Error Damages Community Reputation

**Why it's real:** Land investing communities are high-trust, word-of-mouth environments. A single credibility hit in BiggerPockets or Land Investing Mastery (e.g., "Super Plot missed a wetlands flag, I almost lost $30K") can block Channel 2 for months.

**Mitigation:**
- Manual QA by Tom on every report for the first 50 delivered (before automating)
- Methodology disclosure on every report: "Decision-support range, not a formal appraisal"
- List each comp source, close date, and acreage adjustment — transparency over confidence
- Personal 24-hour response protocol if any error surfaces in a community thread (corrected report + $50 credit)

---

### Risk 3: AcreValue or Lands of America Launches an Overlapping Feature

**Why it's real:** AcreValue, Lands of America, and Zillow already have parcel data and millions of land buyers. An "AI valuation layer" is a 3-month engineering build for a team their size. Distribution moat is real: in-app suggestions need zero new traffic.

**Current competitive gap:** No competitor currently offers fully automated, multi-source (parcel + comps + environmental + valuation) vacant land reports in under 15 minutes. That gap closes.

**Mitigation:**
- Speed is the moat for months 1–6: ship fast, stay 6 months ahead
- Go narrow and deep on rural/vacant; ignore residential, farmland, development land
- Embed Super Plot as the default workflow for 10–20 active wholesalers/brokers — behavioral lock-in
- Every report generated builds a proprietary parcel dataset; 200+ assessments by Sept 2026 = a training data asset competitors cannot replicate quickly
